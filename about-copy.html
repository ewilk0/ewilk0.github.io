Argo launches!

Argo-P

15 JAN 2019

by Byron Butaney

    Argo-p was established by two high school students with a passion for computer science and a desire to assist those less fortunate. Since then, the pair started the organization and began to volunteer at refugee shelters to teach programming, eventually founding the modern day Argo.
