Hi, I'm Ethan Wilk!

 

I'm a teenager from Arizona.

 

I programmed this blog to post my ideas about finance.

The first assets I traded in 2014 were stocks, particularly on breakouts and news. However, as my ability to trade diversified, I found a wider interest in forex. The ability to trade near 24 hours a day, and less limitations particularly in cryptocurrency attracted me more than stocks or index funds.

 

This brought me to this blog, where I discuss my ventures in investing and trading, and program bots to share for all who happen to accidentally find their way to this gem.

 

This blog also has a live trade tab built into it in the navigation bar at the top. You can see some of my trades as they happen up there, entry, exit, and all.

 

Thanks for reading. Let's make some money together!
