Earlier this year, when COVID-19

devastated all corners of society, I realized the potential of advanced technology in alleviating the situation. As such, I organized a team of student programmers and researchers from my school, and we formed the Remidi-19 Emergency Initiative.

 

As part of our operations, we used blockchain and machine learning to predict the spread of COVID-19. With this information, we alerted hospitals in the most at-risk regions, and shipped them our own 3D-printed surgical masks.

 

We were able to enhance our work by partnering with ASU's Official COVID-19 Task Force, and as a result, were invited to be featured in a Forbes documentary set to release this fall.
